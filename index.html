About Us

Founded in 2025 and headquartered in Omaha, NE, Fast Lane Medical was created with one mission in mind: to provide dependable, fast, and secure medical courier services. We specialize in transporting lab specimens, pharmaceuticals, medical equipment, and more. Our team is trained in HIPAA-compliant handling procedures, ensuring every delivery is made with the utmost care.

Led by founder and CEO Jamal Brooks, our company is built on four pillars: Speed, Safety, Care, and Trust. Every delivery we make is a promise kept.

Track Your Delivery in Real Time

We know timing is everything. That’s why we offer real-time delivery tracking to all of our clients. Whether it’s a critical sample or sensitive pharmaceuticals, you’ll always know exactly where your delivery is.

Our Services

We offer fast, secure, and compliant delivery of medical specimens, prescriptions, lab results, equipment, and other healthcare materials throughout Nebraska and beyond.
